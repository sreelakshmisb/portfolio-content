SNOWDEN EXPOSES MASS GOVERNMENT SURVEILLANCE

2013: Intelligence Consultant Edward Snowden leaks highly classified information of the National Security Agency(NSA), showing details of widespread surveillance programs being run by the US government, to spy on its own citizens. The leak fuels debates surrounding government surveillance and raises concerns regarding privacy.


THE HARVEY WEINSTEIN SCANDAL BEGINS TO UNFOLD

2017: The New York Times publishes a story detailing allegations of sexual assault against Hollywood producer Harvey Weinstein. In the days and months that follow, more women come forward and share their accounts of sexual assault involving Weinstein and many others. The event eventually spirals into the worldwide #metoo movement.
